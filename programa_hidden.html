Space Surveillance, From Observations to Services in the Space Industry

DEIMOS SPACE. Noelia Sánchez-Ortiz, PhD, director of SSA department.

Space Situational Awareness (SSA) refers to those activities that intend to tackle risks coming from space (man-made, natural objects and Space weather aspects). The presentation will briefly review the services provided in this domain, higlighting the technical challenges of each processing steps. It will review the end-to-end process, from the observations of space objects to the processing and finally, the services provided to final users.
